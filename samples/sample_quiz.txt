PHẦN 2: BÀI ĐỌC HIỂU
Read the following passage and choose the correct answer for each question:

Computers are important in our modern life. They help us store information, calculate numbers, and connect with people all over the world. Many students use computers for learning and entertainment. However, using computers for too long can be harmful to our eyes.

Câu 3: What do computers help us do?
A. Store information
B. Only play games
C. Sleep better
D. Buy food
Đáp án: A

Câu 4: Who uses computers for learning?
A. Only teachers
B. Many students
C. No one
D. Only old people
Đáp án: B

Câu 5: Using computers for too long is ___ for our eyes.
A. good
B. helpful
C. harmful
D. safe
Đáp án: C
